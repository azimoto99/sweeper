Accessibility Notes

WCAG AA Compliance

  • All text combinations should have a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1
  • Large text (18pt+ or 14pt+ bold) should have at least 3:1 contrast
  • Interactive elements should have clear focus states
  • Color should not be the only way to convey information
